The Proposal:
Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India. The "Central Civil Services (Payment of Gratuity under New Pension System) Rules, 2021" have been implemented as per the notification of the 23.09.2021. Rule 20 provides that the employee's gratuity contribution while on deputation shall be paid to the government by his employer or employee, otherwise the period shall not be treated as duty period for the purpose of contribution. It was under consideration of the government to open a separate avenue for depositing the contribution of gratuity during the period of deputation of officers in the All India Services covered under the new Penhan System (NPS) or Unified Penhan Scheme (UPS) to the government for action as per the said notification.
Government Decision: - In pursuance of the Central Civil Services (Payment of Gratuity under New Pension System) Rules, 2021, "it is hereby approved to open the following Head of Accounts for depositing with the Government the Gratuity during the period of deputation of officers in the All India Services covered under the new USA System NPS or Unified Ua Scheme (UPS): -
