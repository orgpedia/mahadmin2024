Introduction: - Department of Skill Employment Entrepreneurship and Innovation Reference No. 2 Circular and Reference No. 4 Guidelines have been issued regarding the implementation of the Government Muara Apprenticeship Training Scheme. In accordance with this instruction, Reference No. The Government of Maharashtra has approved the recruitment of candidates under the Apprenticeship Training Scheme in Vas Maharashtra State Information Commission, Headquarters and 7 other Benches. As the period of this approval is expiring in March 2026, the State Information Commission vide letter No. 1 has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to take up apprenticeship under the scheme till the scheme is operational. The proposal was under consideration of the government for approval.
Government Decision: - Appointment of a total of 32 apprentice candidates, 4 each in Maharashtra State Information Commission, Headquarters and 7 other Benches under the apprenticeship training scheme is being approved subject to the following conditions as the scheme continues.
1) The number of apprentices will be limited to 25 per cent of the total number of apprentices in the government circular of the Department of Skill Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation.
(2) It shall be mandatory that the budgetary provision required for the payment of scholarships and other payments be available in the relevant financial year.
