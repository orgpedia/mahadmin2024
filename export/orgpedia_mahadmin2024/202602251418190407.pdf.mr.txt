PREAMBLE:-
Under the Election Commission of India (ECI)’s Global Engagement for Electoral
Excellence (GEEE) initiative, a comprehensive exercise has been undertaken to examine
international best practices in electoral management and to disseminate India’s institutional
experience and expertise at the global level. In furtherance of this initiative, specific countries have
been identified and allocated to the respective Chief Electoral Officers (CEOs) of the State for
focused engagement. The State of Maharashtra has been assigned the Republic of Colombia for
this purpose.
Accordingly, as directed by the Commission, a five-member delegation, led by the Chief
Electoral Officer, Maharashtra, shall undertake an official visit to the Republic of Colombia from
6 to 10 March 2026 to advance the objectives of the said initiative. As part of this initiative and as elections for the Colombian Congress are scheduled on 8" March, 2026, Presidente, Consejo
Nacional Electoral, Colombia vide his letter has invited of 5 member delegation headed by Mr S.
Chockalingam, Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra as International Observers in the International
Observation Mission for the elections to the Congress of the Republic. Indian International
Institute of Democracy and Election Management has obtained required clearance for the visit
form Ministry of External Affairs and approval from Election Commission of India and has
communicated to this office vide their letter dated 10™ Feb.2026.
Resolution:-
Taking into consideration the clearance from Ministry of External Affairs and approval
from Election Commission of India, the Government of Maharashtra hereby gives approval to
send Five member delegation headed by Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra to Colombia from
06 March, 2026 to 10 March, 2026. Members of the delegation are as follows:-
